Lionel Messi was born, 24 June 1987, in Rosario, Argentina to a working-class family. His father was a factory steel worker, and his mother a cleaner. He began playing from an early age, and his talent was soon apparent.

However, at the age of 11, Messi was diagnosed with growth hormone deficiency (GHD).
